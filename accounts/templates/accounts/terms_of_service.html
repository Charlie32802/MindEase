4. Platform Scope & Limitations

MindEase is designed to provide:

MindEase is NOT a substitute for emergency services, psychiatric treatment, or clinical diagnosis. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or any immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services or the appropriate crisis hotline immediately.

The platform currently operates as a web-based application and supports English-language interactions only.

5. AI Companion Disclaimer

The MindEase AI Companion is powered by Groq's LLaMA 3.3 70B Versatile model. It is designed to provide conversational support, platform navigation assistance, and counselor recommendations. However:

  • The AI is not a licensed mental health professional and does not provide clinical diagnoses or treatment
  • AI responses are generated by a language model and may occasionally produce inaccurate or inappropriate content
  • The AI may recommend that you speak with a verified counselor based on its assessment of your conversation — this recommendation is algorithmic and not a clinical referral
  • Conversations with the AI are stored in the database and can be cleared by you at any time
